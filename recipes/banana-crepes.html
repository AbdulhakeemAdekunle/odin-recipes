Banana Crepes

Description

Recipe creator Jessican Eymann describes these as, "French crepes filled with a sweet cream sauce over bananas and topped with whipped cream." The cinnamony cream sauce is pure comfort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ cup half-and-half cream
  • 6 bananas, halved lengthwise
  • 1 ½ cups whipped heavy cream
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Step 1: Sift flour and powdered sugar into a mixing bowl. Add eggs, milk, butter, vanilla, and salt; beat until smooth.
  2. Step 2: Heat a lightly greased 6 inch skillet. Add about 3 tablespoons batter. Tilt skillet so that batter spreads to almost cover the bottom of skillet. Cook until lightly browned; turn and brown the other side. Repeat process with remaining batter, grease skillet as needed.
  3. Step 3: Melt 1/4 cup butter in a large skillet. Stir in brown sugar, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir in cream and cook until slightly thickened. Add half the bananas at a time to skillet; cook for 2 to 3 minutes, spooning sauce over them. Remove from heat.
  4. Step 4: Roll a crepe around each banana half and place on serving platter. Spoon sauce over crepes. Top with whipped cream and a pinch of cinnamon.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • 519 Calories
  • 29g Fat
  • 8g Protien
  • 61g Carbs
