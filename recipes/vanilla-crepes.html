Vanilla Crepe

Description

These basic crepes are lightly flavored with vanilla, making them a great base for a variety of sweet toppings. Reviewer kodiano says, "I used Nutella as the filling on the inside...heaven!"

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 5 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: In a large bowl, mix together the milk, egg yolks and vanilla. Stir in the flour, sugar, salt and melted butter until well blended.
  2. Step 2: Heat a crepe pan over medium heat until hot. Coat with vegetable oil or cooking spray. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan and tip to spread the batter to the edges. When bubbles form on the top and the edges are dry, flip over and cook until lightly browned on the other side and edges are golden. Repeat with remaining batter.
  3. Step 3: Fill crepes with your favorite fruit, cream, caramel or even ice cream or cheese to serve.

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

  • 142 Calories
  • 7g Fat
  • 16g Carbs
  • 3g Protein
